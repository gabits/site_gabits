About Me


Gabriela M Valle

Artist and Web Developer

Extremely communicative, easily motivated and quite humorous, I’m a very ethusiastic girl who is passionate about art, architecture and history, and intimately concerned about social matters and the social structures.

When it comes to art, I strongly believe in its critical role but also in the intuitive perception of visual stimulus, and it's why I like to explore the principles of aesthetics and the objective and subjective ideas that can be expressed through art and design.

Being curious but very dissatisfied with the educational system, I do believe in taking pleasure from studying, but specially through computers and internet, wich is also why I began to study coding languages and web development.

I have a constantly thinking and possibly quite creative mind, and it’s usually a personal impulse to find solutions for problems that don’t even exist yet. I'm usually a fast-learner but very detailist person, wich can be both a gift and a challenge for me.
